Client Project // Social Media Campaign

Crush Hydration Drink

The client provided us with the design for a hydration drink, and it was my task to turn it into an ad campaign. I created a series of billboard-style Instagram visuals that showcased the product in a beautiful, nature-inspired setting.

What I Did

• Created realistic mockups from the client's artwork

• Integrated the supplied packaging and branding into the scene

• Enhanced lighting, shadows and realism in Photoshop

• Designed the final visual for social media and advertising

Tools Used

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Premiere Pro
  • After Effects
