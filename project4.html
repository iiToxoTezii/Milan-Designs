Client Project // Product Advertising

Vigra Energy Drink

The client provided the packaging design for an energy drink, and the goal was to transform it into a dynamic visual experience. I integrated the packaging into a realistic mockup and created an animation video that aligns perfectly with the product’s design language, ensuring a cohesive and impactful final result for digital platforms.

What I Did

• Integrated the client’s provided packaging design into realistic product mockups

• Matched the visual environment and elements to align perfectly with the packaging theme

• Created a dynamic animation video that reflects the design language of the product

• Enhanced lighting, motion and overall realism for social media and ad-ready output

Tools Used

  • Adobe After Effects
  • Motion Design & Animation
  • Compositing & Visual Effects
