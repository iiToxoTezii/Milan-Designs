Client Project // Product Mockups

Moonwell Sleeping Gummies

The client provided us with the packaging for Moonwell Sleeping Gummies and briefed us on its purpose and benefits. Keeping that in mind, I integrated the packaging into custom mockups and created visuals that made the product feel natural, calming, and suitable for the brand.

What I Did

• Integrated the client’s packaging into realistic mockups

• Created a calm and natural visual direction matching the product’s purpose

• Used Photoshop and Illustrator to refine lighting, textures and realism

• Designed the final visuals for Instagram and promotional use

Tools Used

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Lightroom
  • Adobe After Effects
