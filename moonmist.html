Client Project // Product Visualization

Moonmist Serum

The client supplied the original packaging design for Moonmist Serum. My role was to transform that flat packaging into a premium and realistic visual. I integrated the supplied label and packaging into mockups, then used Photoshop and Illustrator to create cinematic lighting, reflections, shadows and detailed texture work so the final result looked photorealistic and ready for social media and advertising.

What I Did

• Integrated the client’s supplied packaging into realistic mockups

• Built cinematic lighting, reflections and shadows in Photoshop

• Refined the label and packaging details in Illustrator

• Created premium social-media-ready visuals with a realistic finish

Tools Used

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Mockup Integration
  • Lighting, Texture & Reflection Enhancement
